Wheel Chair R2D2 Droid

At PVHS, I like to tinker around with robots and electronics. I saw this cool robot thing sitting in class and asked my teacher about it. He said, "We got this from Millersville. Some students back in the day tried to get it to be an RC Robot. They fried a couple of componentns and didn't know what they were doing." I took the opportunity to make K.E.M. (Kinematic Engineering Machine)."

The hardawre is just a chassy and motors, salvaged from teh wheel chair. The brain is an Ardiono Mega knockoff, a Vex EDR Cortex, and a couple bread boards for components like 7-segment leds and potentiometers and such. The VEX EDR Cortex is programmed in a flavor of C called RobotC. And the Arduino is a different flavor of C. I am using Packet splicing and the code and resource list can be found here.
