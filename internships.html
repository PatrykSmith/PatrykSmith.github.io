PVHS Interniships

At my highschool they offer internship opportunities. I have the opportunity to work for an organzation called Piercing Word. We are a non-for-profit organization whose motive is to igntite passion for the Word of God in the heart of the Church. We accomplish that through dramatic and muscial presentation of the Word of God, verbatim in the ESV translation of the Holy Bible. The organizaion was founded by Aaron House, Executive Director. If you want more information about Piercing Word and the opportunities they bring, click here! There are also resources and a video below more about my internship and the process I had to go through to get it, and still am going through.

At Piercing Word my responsibilities are to: Work on office work such as: tax exemptions forms, making video trailers for upcoming shows, upload content for PW website, configure sound equipment, take out the trash, clean, interview people, put in hours, etc; Work on Sound Equipment: microphones, receivers; configuring sound boards; testing equipment; sending out old, broken, and damaged equipment; planning out what equipment will be used and distribute them to respected place. At shows: set up audio equipment, run audio, get people involved and make connections, help out with other tech such as video and lights if needed.
